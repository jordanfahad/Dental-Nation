1. Executive Summary

  • Efficiency November spend was dh1,216.78 for 3 leads (CPL dh405.59). From 1 to 18 December, spend increased to dh1,913.39 for 12 leads with CPL improving to dh159.45. Cost per click dropped from dh19.31 to dh9.86 and CPM improved from dh29.55 to dh16.64.
  • Intent Meta campaigns are currently operating as consideration campaigns. CTR is stable around 0.2 percent, which is below the ideal 1 percent plus benchmark for local healthcare. Real high intent volume is expected from Google Search leads campaigns and Google My Business, which will capture patients actively searching for dentists near Al Wasl.
  • Creative The best performing units are clear offer based static ads with strong headline and CTA. However, video ads consistently show better engagement potential. Current stills are not strong enough to push CTR and engagement higher. We need more clinic and doctor based video content, which depends directly on uniforms being delivered.
  • Leads For Al Wasl, four Meta driven leads are already documented in the master sheet (Welcome Check Up, Whitening Flash Offer and Hotel Guest Smile Experience). This confirms that the tracking and receptionist process is working but is still in early volume stage.
  • Next focus Short term focus is to stabilise CPL below dh150, improve lead quality and increase booked appointments once Practo booking and AI contact center flows are live.

5. Strategic Implications & Next Actions

Positioning of Meta Campaigns

Meta (Facebook and Instagram) should continue to be treated as a consideration and awareness engine, not the only source of high intent leads.

  • Maintain Meta campaigns to keep Dental Nation visible in Al Wasl catchment and nurture interest around offers.
  • Accept that CPL will initially be higher while creative and offers are tested, then use these learnings to feed Google campaigns.
  • Use Meta data to identify winning messages for Google Search and GMB, where users already have a strong intent to book.

Immediate Recommendations

  • Scale Google Search Lead campaigns and Google My Business as soon as the landing page under dentalnation.com is approved, to capture high intent traffic.
  • Prioritise production of video ads: clinic walkthrough, doctor introductions and offer based video creatives. Empirically, video has shown better engagement and is critical for improved CTR and CPL.
  • Uniform delivery is now a critical dependency: without uniforms, doctor and staff shoots cannot proceed, which directly limits video content and therefore the performance of Meta campaigns.
  • Continue creative testing: keep offer based static ads as a base, while testing new video angles such as painless treatment, advanced technology and trust based stories.
  • Simplify forms: avoid long instant forms that depress completion, and rely instead on short forms plus WhatsApp conversations.

Once Practo booking and AI contact center flows are fully live, this report can be extended to show lead to booking to revenue by campaign and channel.
