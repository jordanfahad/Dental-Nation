9. Freelancer operating model

This converts the freelancer from a generic designer into a controlled production pod.

Input owned internally

Weekly brief, lane priority, offer details, audience, treatment positioning, CTA, references and brand guardrails.

Execution by freelancer

Offer statics, social posts, AI videos, reels, raw footage edits, resized variations and paid media adaptations.

Approval owned internally

Brand alignment, medical sensitivity, price/offer accuracy, final visual approval and channel publishing decision.

Meeting close: Approve the designer, confirm Influencer Agency I/O inputs, and lock the weekly content production cadence.
